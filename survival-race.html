    Survival Race

    What is the Survival Race game?

    Survival Race is an intense multiplayer racing game where players battle for supremacy in an unstable arena of crumbling hexagons. The objective is to outlast other players through strategic driving, collecting power-ups, and ramming opponents out of the arena. With its fast-paced action, the game challenges players to stay alive in a constantly shifting environment, combining elements of speed, combat, and survival. Its competitive nature and thrilling gameplay make it a fun and challenging experience for racing enthusiasts.

    How to Play

    • Arrow keys (or WASD) to move
    • Space to jump
    • Press Space again to fly
